Notes

Interaction wireframe, styled to the current OISY Contacts modal. Starts on the user menu — click "Notes" (added right under "Contacts") to open the Notes modal. Close it with X, the Close button, or by clicking the dimmed area (which returns to the menu); the editor step has no X and ignores outside clicks. The avatar (top-right) reopens the menu. Each saved note row then shows a pencil (edit) and a trash (delete) action on the right. Deleting shows a pinned, auto-dismissing "Note deleted / Undo" snackbar at the bottom (never lost to list scroll). The add/edit editor is a **full-page step** inside the modal — a step in the floating card on desktop, and full-screen on mobile (matching OISY Contacts) — which is more keyboard-robust than a bottom sheet for text entry. Toggle "Simulate: at note cap" to see the disabled add + cap banner (editing and deleting stay enabled). Demo character limit lowered to 100 to show validation (real limit 2,000, error-only).
